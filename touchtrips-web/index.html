About
Touchtrips
Touchtrips connects elite travelers to quality businesses. By targeting frequent travelers, we can help bring local bars, restaurants, and tour providers a customer base with higher budgets and higher standards. Our platform offers users a streamlined way to organize their free time while traveling. Using our beautiful app, they can book restaurants, bars, and cultural activities with the touch of a button.
The airlines take you there, the hotels provide the beds, but Touchtrips gets your further. My goal is for Touchtrips to offer a world of personalized travel experiences at your destination. Creating memorable, yet convenient experiences whether you're traveling on business or for leisure.

For partners
We work closely with our bar, restaurant, and tour provider partners to deliver the best possible experience for our platform users. We also provide each of our partners with a comprehensive service, complete with technological support, media exposure, training, and quality checks, so that working with Touchtrips is as easy and straightforward as using our app.
